About the movie

"Call me by your name" is a movie by Director Luca Guadagnio, starring Timothee Chalamet as Elio and Armi Hammer as Oliver. The movie was nominated for the best picture for the Academy award and has won several awards as well.The film was adapted by a book with the same name which was written by Andre Aciman.
